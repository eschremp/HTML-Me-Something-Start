Apple Pie

ingredients
56 g butter
13 g flour
28 g water
3 g cornstarch
8 g vanilla extract
55 g white sugar
55 g brown sugar
3 g cinnamon
dash nutmeg
375 g apples
  1. Preheat oven to 425 °F. Prepare the pan and bottom crust.
  2. Melt butter in a pan. Add flour and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Mix the water and cornstarch. Add to pan.
  4. Add the sugars and spices. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Combine apples and sauce. Pour into prepared pie pan.
  6. Add top crust and bake for 25 minutes
  7. Remove from oven. Let cool and enjoy!

Brownies

ingredients
6 oz butter
4 oz chocolate bar
400 g brown sugar
3 eggs (room temperature)
2 t vanilla extract
82 g cocoa powder
1 T instant coffee
125 g flour
1 t salt
225 g chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9x13 pan.
  2. Melt butter and half the chocolate bar. Once melted, whisk in brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix. Add everything else.
  4. Spread into prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes
  5. Remove from oven. Let cool and enjoy!
